## KEY TAKEAWAYS
================================================================================

1. **Integrated Platform**: Odoo MRP connects all manufacturing processes on
   a single real-time database, eliminating information silos.

2. **Flexibility**: Multiple manufacturing strategies (MTO, MTS, ETO) and
   configurations (1, 2, or 3 steps) adapt to business needs.

3. **Complete Traceability**: Lot and serial number tracking provides full
   visibility from raw materials to finished goods.

4. **Cost Visibility**: Real-time cost tracking (materials, labor, overhead)
   provides immediate profitability insights.

5. **Mobile-First**: Barcode scanning and shop floor tablet interface designed
   for manufacturing floor efficiency.

6. **Quality Integration**: Built-in quality control points and maintenance
   management prevent issues before they occur.

7. **Scalability**: From simple one-step manufacturing to complex multi-level
   BoMs with subcontracting, the system grows with your business.

8. **Data-Driven**: Extensive reporting and analysis tools enable continuous
   improvement and informed decision-making.
